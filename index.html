RULES

Scoring for the game works as follows:

  • The player plays against the computer by pressing the Start Game/Next Roll button
  • There are three rounds or two dice each. Each round is scored for both players
  • If the player rolls a one (1) on either dice the score for that round is zero
  • If the player rolls a pair dice the score for that round is the total of the pair times two
  • If the player rolls anything else, the score for the round is the total of the two dice
